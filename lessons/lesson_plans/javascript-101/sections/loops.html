Comparison and Logical Operators

Comparison Operators
Comparison operators use logical statements to determine differences between variables.

Using comparison operators we can determine the values of variables. Are the variables equal? Is one variable greater than or lesser than another. Once we've determined the value of a variable or its contents we can then take action based on conditions.

Logical Operators
Logical operators are used to evaluate multiple conditions within a conditional statement.
