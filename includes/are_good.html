RhinoCoders: teaching front-end web design

RhinoCoders will immerse the youth of Metropolitan Detroit into the world of interactive architecture by providing them with a digital foundation from which to grow from through a series of fun and interactive tracks instructed by SapientNitro Detroit. Students will learn how to code efficiently, develop stunning websites, create engaging apps and discover the latest cutting-edge technologies.
