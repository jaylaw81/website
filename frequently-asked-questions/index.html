Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What makes RhinoCoders educational?

A: Code is now thought of as a new language and not all languages are taught in schools. With that, RhinoCoders will be teaching attendee's the learning blocks to code. Some of the fundamentals that will be taught include math, geometry and art.

Q: Where are classes held?

A: Currently, classes will be held at SapientNitro Detroit.

Q: How long are classes?

A: Classes are 2 hours long (Two 45 minute sessions with two 15 minute breaks).

Q: How much to classes cost?

A: All classes are free! RhinoCoders is providing the courses as a service, to the youth of Metro Detroit.

Q: What do I need to bring?

A: RhinoCoders provides all equipment and session materials. You will also receive a thumb drive with a DropBox account installed on it for saving work. All accounts are to be setup utilizing parents account.

Q: Who can participate in a class?

A: Classes are geared towards children ages 7-17. Parents are welcomed and encouraged to attend with their children to help move them through the coursework and provide guidance along the way.
