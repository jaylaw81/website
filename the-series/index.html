The Series

Having fun is priority numero uno.

We are providing a FREE series on web design and development for ages 7—100 called "The Basics". Ask yourself, “Do I have a pulse and a burning desire to unlock the mysteries of web development?” If you answered yes to either of these questions, please sign up! We would love to have you! This series will walk students through a brief history of HTML, how to create a basic web page using it, how to style a web page with CSS and how to add interactivity with JavaScript. We'll reveal other extraordinary "secrets" including the software that is necessary to develop a website. The series takes three (3) weeks to complete and is broken down as follows:

Classes are held on Saturdays, from 9:00am to 2:00pm at SapientNitro 135 N. Old Woodward, Birmingham, MI 48009.

Week 1: HTML

Week 2: CSS

Week 3: JavaScript

Only students need a ticket (parents/guardians can attend without a ticket). Computers will be provided to the students. Registering for this series reserves the student a seat in the class for all three (3) weeks. Registering also assumes the student will be in attendance for all three (3) classes. If a week is missed, we will begrudgingly make accommodations to ensure that the student has a chance to learn the materials missed.

Please bring a lunch (food will not be provided—our lawyer won’t let us feed you. We know, not cool…)
